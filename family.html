What is Family Protection Life Insurance?

This is a life insurance policy that provides financial benefits to your dependents in case of death, disability, or critical illness. It ensures your family continues to live well, even if life takes an unexpected turn.

Why Every Family Needs It

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Covers school fees, rent, daily expenses, and more
  • 💔 Prevents financial struggle in case of sudden death
  • 💼 Helps repay outstanding debts or mortgages
  • ❤️ Leaves a lasting legacy for your loved ones

Key Benefits

  • ✅ Affordable monthly premiums
  • ✅ Flexible duration and sum assured
  • ✅ Critical illness & accidental death benefits
  • ✅ Immediate claims assistance
  • ✅ Tax-free payout to nominated beneficiary

How It Works

  1. Choose a sum assured (coverage amount)
  2. Select premium mode (monthly or annual)
  3. Get covered immediately after underwriting
  4. Your family receives payout upon valid claim
